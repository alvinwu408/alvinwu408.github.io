中英哲言     

中文名言   英文名言

　

　

1.   A rose by any other name would smell as sweet.

2.   Cowards die many times before their deaths; valiant never taste of death but once.

3.   It is curious that physical courage should be so common in the world and moral courage so rare.

4.   Man is the only animal that blushes. Or needs to.

5.   The only way to keep your health is to eat what you don’t want, drink what you don’t like, do what you’d rather not.

6.   In war there is no substitute for victory.

7.   Man is not made for defeat. A man can be destroyed but not defeated.

8.   Pain does not matter to a man.

9.   Life is indeed valuable; even higher is the price of love; if for the sake of liberty, both can be sacrificed.

10.   He that is god for making excuses is seldom good for anything else.

11.   It is easier to suppress the first desire than to satisfy all that follow it.

12.   Ask not what your country can do for you, ask what you can do for your country.

13.   Mankind must put an end to war or war will put an end to mankind.

14.   I don't wait for good moods. You accomplish nothing if you do that. Your mind must know it has got to get down to work.

15.   Every great mistake has a half-way moment, a split second when it can be recalled and perhaps remedied.

16.   "Impossible" is a word to be found only in the dictionary of fools.

17.   A well-educated man should know something of everything and everything of something.

18.   To err is human; to forgive, divine.

19.   Genius is one percent inspiration and ninety-nine percent perspiration.

20.   Some people think that doctors and nurses can put scrambled eggs back into the shell.

21.   A man travels the world over in searching of what he needs and returns home to find it.

22.   Nothing will ever be attempted if all possible objections must first be overcome.

23.   If winter comes, can spring be far behind?

24.   No winter lasts forever; no spring skips its turn.

25.   Politicians are the same all over. They promise to build a bridge even where there is no river.

26.   It's funny thing about life; if you refuse to accept anything but the best, you very often get it.

27.   If you cannot catch a bird of paradise, better take a wet hen.

28.   No man ever yet became great by imitation.

29.   It is the last straw that breaks the camel's back.

30.   Measure your health by your sympathy with morning and spring.

31.   Never hesitate to hold out your hand; never hesitate to accept the outstretched hand of another.

32.   When woman love us, they forgive us everything even our crimes; when they do not love us, they give us credit for nothing, not for our virtues.

33.   "Whenever you feel like criticizing anyone," he told me "just remember that all the people in this world haven't had the advantages that you've had."
