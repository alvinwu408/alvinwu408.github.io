Whats an Audit?
An audit is where we go through everyones points and make sure they are correct. We take points out that should not be in the system. In addition we have to wait for revenue confirmation from our largest advertisers. This can take from 45-60 days following the end of the month.

什麼是審核?
所謂審核是我們會審查所有人的點數並且確定它們是正確的. 我們會將會把不該屬於系統的點數刪除. 另外, 我們必須等待大量廣告商的收入項目認可. 這可能會需要從月底算起45至60天的時間.

Why did my Audited numbers go down?
We remove the points from cheaters from the entire pool and from everyones downline. We also modify accounts that have inconsistant sets of data.

為什麼我的已審核點數會減少?
我們會把作弊者的點數從整個聯結以及所有人的下線中刪除. 我們也會更正帳戶中不符合的日期.
